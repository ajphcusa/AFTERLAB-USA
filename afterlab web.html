So… what is konjac jelly?

Konjac is a plant-based fiber used across Asian-style jelly snacks for its smooth, bouncy texture. It's what gives AFTERLAB its signature chew while keeping calories low.

We blend it with real fruit concentrate and a hero beauty ingredient, then seal it into a single-serve pouch — a sweet, refreshing treat that actually fits a mindful routine.

What is AFTERLAB Konjac Jelly?+
A sweet, chewy jelly snack made with konjac and a hero beauty ingredient — collagen, glutathione, or biotin — plus real fruit concentrate. It's a light, refreshing pouch you can enjoy any time you want something sweet.
What does it taste like?+
Fruity and refreshing with a smooth, bouncy jelly texture. Pomegranate, apple mango, and green apple each taste like the real fruit. It's best chilled.
Is it really low calorie?+
Yes — 7 kcal (Biotin), 10 kcal (Collagen), and 15 kcal (Glutathione) per 150g pouch, with a low-sugar formula. Check the label for full nutrition facts.
Should I refrigerate it?+
You can enjoy it at room temperature, but most people prefer it chilled — the texture and flavor feel more refreshing straight from the fridge.
Is this only for people dieting?+
Not at all. It's for anyone who wants a fun, sweet, refreshing snack. Some people use it as a lighter alternative to candy or dessert, but everyone's welcome.
